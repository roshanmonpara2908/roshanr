Popular Destinations

London

Enjoy the fantastic sights and events in London and make the most of the capital's many things to do.

Whether you are a first-time visitor or a Londoner born and bred, you’ll discover what to do in London and plenty of things to see, from the best London tourist attractions to unusual ideas for days out in London.
As summer reaches it’s peak, find thrilling things to do at London’s outdoor attractions, or soak up the last of the summer sun at relaxing green spaces and alfresco hotspots.
If you’re looking for a fun-filled day out in the capital, there’s still plenty of seasonal celebrations to catch – from epic music festivals and summer pop-ups to open-air events in the heart of the city.
Get your culture fix and discover the wonders of London museums, savour delicious traditional dishes and see London sights from a new perspective on a London tour.
Use the London attractions map to help pinpoint all the places you want to go around the capital.
